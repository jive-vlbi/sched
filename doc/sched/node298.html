Version 7.0

This is the current development version.

  • Add parameter EVLA to allow the VLA to reach frequencies allowed by the EVLA but not the VLA. Sept. 26, 2007. RCW.

  • Convert all stations to Mark5 in the station catalog. Nov. 8, 2006 RCW

  • Found and fixed bug in the calculation of station parallax for satellite positions. March 26, 2007. RCW.

  • Put indicator of continuous recording in .sch file. May 8, 2007.

  • Increase maximum number of setups. June 8, 2007 RCW.

  • Add setup parameter M4PATCH and subroutine chk4geo.f to support geodetic type setups. This is incomplete, but is at least making a polarization study of the geodetic stations (VLBA providing both polarizations) possible. June 2007 RCW.

  • Add 24GHz band. Aug 28, 2007

  • Put SCHED  under revision control with SVN. Adapted Cormac Reynolds version that had about a 2 year history. Aug 31, 2007. RCW.
