PINAME

PINAME is the name of the Principal Investigator. Actually, the person who should be used here should be the one who is organizing the project, not necessarily the one leading the proposal. With 64 characters, more than one person could be given. SCHED  will abort if PINAME is not given for tape recording observations. This is for the cover information.

  • Argument: Text of up to 64 characters.
  • Options: Any.
  • Default: Blank, causing SCHED  to abort.
  • Usage: Only one value used, the last.
  • Example: PINAME='E.T. Smith'
