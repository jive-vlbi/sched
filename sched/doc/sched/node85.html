EMAIL

EMAIL gives the principal Investigator's electronic mail address for the cover information. Use an Internet address where possible. Either of EMAIL or FAX must be provided if recording tape. Both should be provided.

  • Argument: Text of up to 64 characters.
  • Options: Any.
  • Default: Blank
  • Usage: Only one value used, the last.
  • Example: EMAIL=cwalker@nrao.edu
