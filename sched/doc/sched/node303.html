Release of 26 June 1996

  • Some small format and manual changes 14apr96.

  • Fix bug with FORMAT passed to CHKSPD - problem with mixed modes. 17apr96.

  • Fix bug with obscode default when tapeini not specified 18apr96.

  • Fix bug with SCANS optimization mode not going full time. 19apr96.

  • Reverse sign of U in plots for normal convention. 23apr96.

  • Put tapeini and pass times in summary file. 23apr96.

  • Add TSETTLE to station catalog. 25apr96

  • Add INDXHEAD and HEADPOS to simplify VEX additions. 25apr96

  • Alter defaulting of FE to avoid need for 'OMIT'. 25apr96

  • Remove all INTEGER*2 to prevent confusion and because nonstandard. 26apr96

  • Add much checking of VLA parameters (CHKVLA and BADLO) 29apr96.

  • Get rid of ALLVLBA option (produce VLBA type cards for all stations. 01 May 96

  • Split STAFILES out of main routine. 01 May 96.

  • Add calls to VEX routines, DOVEX input, and beginning Mark IV support. 01 May 96.

  • Remove MARK3 and VLBATP logicals - add VLBITP. Make OBSTYP local to SCHIN. 02May96

  • Add routine CHKVLBA with checks of FE, SYNTH etc. 3May96

  • Set unused synthesizers to 10.9 GHz for 2 cm observations.

  • Add EARLY and DWELL to SUMITEM options. 6May96.

  • Add correlator parameters. 14 May 96.

  • Add DAR, RECORDER, NBBC, NDRIVEs, NHEADs to station catalog 17 May 1996.

  • Add much stuff about VLA defaults. Print to summary. 22May1996

  • Add planets and JPL ephemeris. Bryan Butler and RCW 26 Jun 96
